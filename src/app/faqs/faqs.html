Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions.

How do I submit an assignment?

+

Go to the Assignments section, select your task, complete it, and click Submit Assignment before the deadline.

How can I upload my project?

+

Navigate to the Projects page and click the Upload button to submit your project files.

Where can I download my certificate?

+

After successfully completing your course, your certificate will be available in the Certificates section.

How do I reset my password?

+

Click on Forgot Password from the login page, enter your registered email address, and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

How can I contact support?

+

Visit the Raise Ticket page, describe your issue, attach any required files, and submit your request. Our support team will contact you as soon as possible.
